About EFL
The Tournament

The English Football League (EFL) is the annual intra-departmental football tournament organized by students of the Department of English, Jahangirnagar University. Now entering its 4th season, EFL has grown into a celebrated tradition that brings together football enthusiasts from all batches.

Season History
Season 1
Inaugural edition. The tradition begins by Batch 48.
Season 2
Expanded format with more teams and players.
Season 3
Record participation and memorable finals.
Season 4 — Current
Now with dedicated digital platform introduced by Batch 51.
Tournament Rules
01
Eligibility:
All currently enrolled students of the Dept. of English, JU are eligible to participate.
02
Squad Size:
Each team must register a minimum of 7 and a maximum of 10 players.
03
Format:
League format followed by knockout semi-finals and a grand final.
04
Match Duration:
Each match consists of two halves as determined by the organizing committee.
05
Fair Play:
All players are expected to maintain sportsmanship and respect for opponents and officials.
06
Disputes:
All disputes shall be referred to the organizing committee whose decision is final.
